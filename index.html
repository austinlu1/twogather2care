Our Mission

Why We Show Up

Many elders in nursing homes face isolation and inactivity. We believe students can change that — bringing energy, empathy, and community right to their door.

🧠

Mental Wellness

Conversations, games, and social interaction help combat loneliness and cognitive decline.

💪

Physical Activity

Light exercises, dance, and movement sessions keep residents active and energized.

🎉

Joyful Events

Holiday celebrations, arts & crafts, music sessions, and trivia nights that everyone looks forward to.

🤝

Real Connection

Building genuine relationships between students and elders — not just a one-time visit.

What You'll Do

How Volunteers Make a Difference

No experience required — just a willingness to show up and care. Here's what a typical visit looks like:

1

Recreational Activities

Lead board games, card games, puzzles, or craft sessions that residents can enjoy at their own pace.

2

Group Exercise & Movement

Guide gentle chair yoga, stretching, or light dance routines tailored to residents' abilities.

3

Conversation & Companionship

Simply sit, listen, and share stories. Your presence alone is enormously meaningful.

4

Special Events & Celebrations

Help plan and run holiday parties, birthday events, and seasonal themed activities.

5

Music & Arts

Sing-alongs, music listening sessions, painting, and creative expression for all skill levels.

6

Fundraising Support

Help organize drives and events that fund supplies, activities, and facility improvements.

Fundraising

Ready to Volunteer?

Joining is simple. Here's how to get started with TwoGather2Care:

Reach Out

Email us at twogather2care@gmail.com to express your interest and ask any questions.

Get Oriented

Attend a short orientation to learn about our nursing home partners, expectations, and safety guidelines.

Schedule a Visit

Sign up for upcoming volunteer sessions that fit your schedule — flexible timing available.

Show Up & Care

Arrive, connect with residents, and make a real difference in someone's day.
